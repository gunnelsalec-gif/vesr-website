Why Cybersecurity Matters

Healthcare organizations manage sensitive patient data. Cyber threats like ransomware and data breaches pose serious risks to privacy and operations.

My Role

IT Support Specialist helping staff with EHR access, devices, and basic network issues so clinical teams can focus on patient care.

Ethical Issue

Handling PHI: even when repairing devices, avoid unnecessary viewing of patient info and keep all data private.

Best Practices

  • Data Encryption: Protect data at rest and in transit.
  • Access Control: MFA and role-based access.
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and risk evaluations.
  • Employee Training: Phishing awareness, password hygiene, incident response.
  • Patch Management: Keep software up to date.
